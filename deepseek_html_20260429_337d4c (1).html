Our Story

Two lives,
one love

It started with a wrong coffee order and a borrowed umbrella on a rainy Tuesday in London. What was supposed to be a brief apology became a six-hour conversation that neither of us wanted to end.

Seven years, three countries, and more memories than we can count — now we return to the Tuscan hillside where James got down on one knee, this time to say "I do".
